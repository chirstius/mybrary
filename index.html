Features

📷

Scan to Add

Point your camera at any book's barcode and Mybrary fills in the details automatically.

📊

Google Sheets Sync

Your library lives in a Google Sheet you own — export, share, or back it up any time.

📤

Share Books

Send a book card to friends. They can tap the link to add it to their own Mybrary.

🏷️

Track Everything

Reading status, progress, notes, location, tags, loans — all in one place.

📚

Multiple Libraries

Separate shelves for home, office, kids' books — switch between them instantly.

🔔

Loan Tracking

Record who borrowed a book and when it's due. Get overdue alerts at a glance.
